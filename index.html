About NxT Job

The NxT job site queries different public APIs for its data therefore offering the user the best job opportunities around, currently the project uses Adzuna public API for its data but there are plans in the furure to include other available APIs.

Our Story

The NxT project was initiated by the founder Enock Mokua as part of a school project, originally the website was only meant to provide job opportunities in the computer programming field while offering lessons that could complement the skills reqired in the jobs. The NxT project has grown to offer careers in the technology field and even offering short term opportunities such as freelancing options.
